Questions answered

Mock test FAQ

How is the test conducted?
Via video call — Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp Video, whichever you prefer. You don't need to install anything special. Just a phone or laptop with a good internet connection.
Is this a real IELTS Speaking test format?
Yes. The test follows the exact official IELTS Speaking format — Part 1 (introduction & interview), Part 2 (long turn / cue card), and Part 3 (discussion). Duration is 11–14 minutes, same as the real exam.
How accurate is the band score estimate?
Very accurate. Our trainer is an IDP IELTS certified professional who has conducted hundreds of speaking assessments. Students consistently find the estimated band matches their actual exam result very closely.
How do I pay?
Payment details are shared when you book via WhatsApp. We accept common methods including UPI (for India), Interac/e-transfer (for Canada), and international options. We'll make it easy for you.
What if I want the video private but also want coaching?
Premium Pro is exactly for you — full feedback, 10-minute pre-test coaching, and private delivery. Your video is never uploaded to YouTube without your permission.
Can I book multiple mock tests to track my progress?
Absolutely — and we recommend it. Many students book 2–3 mock tests spaced over their preparation period to track improvement and fine-tune their performance before exam day.
