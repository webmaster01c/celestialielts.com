Teaching philosophy

Why 1-on-1 always wins.

🎯

No wasted time

In a batch class, you spend 90% of the time learning things you already know. 1-on-1 means every minute targets your actual weak spots.

🧠

Strategy over memorisation

I don't teach you to memorise answers. I teach you the examiner's mindset — what they're looking for, and how to give it to them every time.

❤️

It's personal

I genuinely care about each student's journey. Your PR, your university offer, your nursing registration — these matter to me as much as they do to you.
