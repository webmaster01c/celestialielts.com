Who is IELTS for?

🎓 Students

Applying to universities in the UK, Australia, Canada, USA or New Zealand. IELTS Academic is the standard requirement.

✈️ Immigrants

Seeking PR in Australia, Canada (Express Entry), or UK Skilled Worker visa — IELTS General Training meets the requirement.

🏥 Healthcare professionals

Nurses and doctors registering with NMC (UK) or AHPRA (Australia) often require IELTS Academic with Band 7+ in all modules.

💼 Professionals

Working professionals needing to demonstrate English proficiency for job applications or career advancement abroad.

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to get my target score?
Most students see significant improvement in 5 days to a month with focused 1-on-1 sessions. The timeline depends on your current level, target band, and availability for sessions. We'll give you a clear timeline estimate after your free consultation.
What's the difference between IELTS Academic and General Training?
IELTS Academic is for university admissions and professional registration (nursing, medicine). IELTS General Training is for immigration, work visas, and permanent residency. Both have the same Listening and Speaking tests but different Reading and Writing tasks.
Should I take IELTS or CELPIP for Canadian PR?
Both are accepted by IRCC (Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada). CELPIP is fully computer-based and results come faster. Many of our students find CELPIP's interface easier to manage. We can advise you based on your specific profile — just WhatsApp us.
Do you offer classes at flexible times for people in Canada or the UK?
Yes — we specifically accommodate timezone differences. We offer early morning, evening, and weekend slots for students in Canada (EST/PST), UK (GMT/BST), and UAE (GST). Your schedule is our schedule.
How does 1-on-1 coaching work online?
Sessions are conducted via video call (Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp Video — your preference). You get personalised attention, real-time feedback, and structured practice tasks between sessions. Everything is tailored to your specific needs.
Do you guarantee a specific band score?
While no ethical coach can guarantee a specific band (the exam board does the testing!), our track record speaks for itself. We've helped hundreds of students reach their target bands — you can see their results on our Success Stories page.
