Quick answers

Common questions.

What happens in a free consultation?
It's a relaxed 15-minute chat via WhatsApp or video call. We'll ask about your current level, your target band, your exam date, and your schedule — then give you an honest plan for how to get there. Zero sales pressure.
How quickly can I start classes?
Often within 24–48 hours of your first message. We don't have long waitlists or complicated onboarding. Just reach out and we'll sort a time that suits you.
Do you teach on weekends?
Yes. We know most students are working professionals or students with weekday commitments. Weekend and early morning or late evening slots are available across all timezones.
What platform are classes held on?
Zoom, Google Meet, or WhatsApp Video — your choice. You don't need to install anything special. Any smartphone or laptop with a decent internet connection works perfectly.
I'm not sure which test to take — can you help?
Absolutely — this is one of the most common questions we get. Just WhatsApp us with your goal (Canada PR, UK nursing, Australian university, etc.) and we'll tell you exactly which test to take and why, in about 2 minutes.
Do you offer any free resources?
Yes! Our YouTube channel has free speaking mock test recordings, student reviews, and IELTS tips. Our Telegram channel has daily vocabulary and speaking prompts. And Spotify has podcast-style IELTS advice. All free, always.
